Sentinel-AI
Privacy-Preserving AI Infrastructure for Secure Enterprise Workflows
“AI sees the context — not the secrets.”

🚀 Overview
Sentinel-AI is a privacy-preserving AI platform that enables organizations to safely use powerful AI systems on confidential documents without exposing sensitive information.
The platform automatically:
detects sensitive entities,
sanitizes confidential data using intelligent placeholders,
enables secure AI processing,
and restores the original context afterward through a reversible rehydration engine.
Sentinel-AI bridges the gap between:
AI Intelligence ⚡ + Enterprise Privacy 🔒

🛡️ Problem Statement
Organizations in healthcare, finance, legal, HR, and government sectors cannot safely leverage cloud-based AI systems because transmitting Protected Health Information (PHI), Personally Identifiable Information (PII), and regulated enterprise data may violate compliance frameworks such as:
HIPAA
GDPR
SOC2
PCI-DSS
Traditional redaction tools permanently remove sensitive information, destroying semantic context and reducing AI effectiveness.
Sentinel-AI solves this through a privacy-preserving AI workflow that protects sensitive data while preserving contextual intelligence.

✨ Core Features
🔍 AI-Powered Sensitive Data Detection
Automatically identifies:
Personal Information
Financial Data
Medical Records
Enterprise Identifiers
Government IDs
Confidential Business Information

🔒 Intelligent Placeholder Sanitization
Example:
Rahul Verma earns ₹18,50,000 at Infosys.

becomes:
[PERSON_1] earns [MONEY_1] at [ORG_1].


♻️ Reversible Context Restoration
AI-generated outputs are securely restored using placeholder mappings.
Example:
[PERSON_1] appears financially stable.

becomes:
Rahul Verma appears financially stable.


🤖 Secure AI Workflow
Sentinel-AI enables organizations to safely use AI systems for:
medical analysis,
legal review,
enterprise intelligence,
financial assessment,
and document summarization
without exposing real confidential data.

🛡️ Prompt Injection Protection
Prevents malicious AI prompts from extracting sensitive information during AI inference.

📊 Compliance-Aware Privacy Layer
Designed for privacy-sensitive industries aligned with:
HIPAA
GDPR
SOC2
PCI-DSS

🏗️ Workflow
Upload Confidential Documents
            ↓
AI-Powered PII Detection
            ↓
Dynamic Placeholder Sanitization
            ↓
Secure AI Processing
            ↓
Placeholder-Aware AI Output
            ↓
Secure Context Restoration
            ↓
Final Enterprise Insights


🧪 Example Workflow
Original Input
Sarah Mitchell underwent an MRI scan at Apollo Hospital.

Sanitized Output
[PERSON_1] underwent an MRI scan at [ORG_1].

AI Response
[PERSON_1]'s scan does not indicate acute abnormalities.

Restored Output
Sarah Mitchell's scan does not indicate acute abnormalities.


🎯 Use Cases
Healthcare AI Workflows
Legal Document Analysis
Financial Risk Assessment
HR & Payroll Processing
Enterprise AI Security
Government Compliance Systems

🔥 What Makes Sentinel-AI Unique?
Unlike traditional redaction systems, Sentinel-AI:
preserves semantic meaning,
enables reversible AI workflows,
protects sensitive enterprise data,
and allows AI systems to operate without accessing real confidential information.
“AI understands the meaning — without seeing the secrets.”
